Scotland's designated bathing waters mainly cover beaches, with a small number of inland sites. Bathing water is monitored for pollution, visible litter, and sewage-related debris.

Data on bathing water quality last updated April 2026.

During the bathing water season, daily water quality predictions are published on SEPA's bathing water pages.

Data: SEPA. Classifications under the Bathing Waters (Scotland) Regulations 2008.
